Divination Card
Item class
(?)
Also referred to as:
Path of Exile Awakening: What Are DIVINATION CARDS? - How Divination Cards Work & Where they Drop
A
divination card
is a type of
item
that can be collected to exchange for a reward. There are many different cards, each one dropping in a different part of the game and granting a different reward.
Overview
Divination cards drop from monsters and loot containers. They are stackable, tradeable items that take up 1x1 inventory space. A full set of cards can be vendored to
Tasuni
in
Highgate
or
Lilly Roth
at your
hideout
in exchange for the item referenced on the card. Each card type drops in specific areas of
Wraeclast
, so farming said area allows for the player to maximize their chances of obtaining the desired item. Additionally, divination cards are affected by the
drop rate penalty
.
Character level is never considered to be higher than 68 in regards to
drop rate penalty
, meaning that high level characters (68+) can farm
world areas
in later acts without severe drop rate penalties. For example if the monster level is 66 there is a 0% drop rate penalty, and in zones with level 56 monsters there is a 25% drop rate penalty.
Rewards
Divination cards that award an item whose level depends on the player's level, are capped at
item level
80.
If a set of divination cards awards a
corrupted
item, then the corruption effect is guaranteed to be an
implicit
mod with the exception of maps, which cannot have implicit mods.
League-specific items
can
be obtained from divination cards with a generic description.

Rampage
-exclusive drop, can be acquired by turning in

. This is only true for items that dropped league-wide. Items that only drop from specific monsters, such as
Atziri
-exclusive unique items or
Breachlord
rewards, or items that only dropped from opening league-specific loot containers, such as

later decided that the text had to be changed to avoid copyright infringement. The current text was written by Lantador himself.
The card's art is based on photographs of Lantador's late wife.
