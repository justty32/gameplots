Kraken are a rare but terrifying sight for any sailor, and a potent weapon when commanded by an Overlord's priest. Mistform may prove quite unsettling. Smoking woods sometimes spawn mistforms, who may infest your empire, running around, killing  the workers and pillaging the improvements.
This is a rare, one time event, and the game advise you with a message about something you see in the smoke.
Troubles come form the
invisibility
of the Mistform: you don't see it, you may not attack it, and workers are not aware of the presence and continue working, stupidly, as other workers around them fall killed by the mist.
Moreover the Mistorm are quite smart and seem disinclined to suicide themselves against the walls of the cities.
You have to immediately retreat the workers in the cities and move any unit under heavy guard, and organize a mean to see the invisible, like training a
hawk
.
