Strategy
Treat your Angels like Great People that you can't settle until they're upgraded. Normally around this stage of the game when they come into play, Angels are weak and vulnerable to many enemies, especially those Assasins that would normally go after those magi instead. Keep in mind that the more experienced the Angel is, the more options of upgrading it will have as well.
An interesting and overlooked fact is that if The Pyre of the Seraphic is accesible to you, Angels can sacrifice themsleves there to become Fire Elementals.

Odio was the once powerful Archangel of the Angel of Rage, Aeron. After the Fall of Agares the Angel of Hope and now Despair, Aeron became the Angel of Strength to the Angel of Rage.
When the One sundered the path of Heaven and Erebus all the angels except Agares created armies of angels that were loyal to them. They also created an angel that would be second in command other than themselves, this was called an "Archangel". Then when the Gods began the great battle called the "Gods War", all the angels fought to the death.
Several of the neutral Gods saw what the war was doing to Erebus. They stood up against the rest of the Angels and threatened to attack anyone that wanted to wage more war. After seeing this the other gods obliged and created the Compact, but Odio wasn't finished with the war yet and unleashed an army of Ira onto the forces of the Angel of Earth, Kilmorph. Outraged by this Kilmorph quickly confronted the other Angels, and not wanting to start another gods war, everyone decided that Odio violated the Compact and should be punished for it. So Kilmorph grabbed Odio by the foot and pull him into the earth and eternally turned him into stone.
To this day the place where Odio was petrified can be seen, people who lived close by named it Odio's Prison. It is not known whether Aeron has a new Archangel or not, or whether Odio's punishment will ever end.
