Gargabol
is a unique
Boss creature
. He is of the
Eldritch faction
. He is required to be killed in the quest
Cleanse the Corruption
. His boss arena has a unique name in the
Tainted Wood
called "Heart of the Forest". To challenge this boss you will need to bring a
Skeleton Key
.
It is a 2 phase boss fight. Phase 1 has Gargabol use acid/poison attacks. Once its HP is depleted, it revives as and begins to use fire attacks, in addition to spawning adds.

Golems are a
creature
type belonging to the
Eldritch
race. They take the form of giant animated rock creatures.
Golems possess relatively high Armor and Health, and have high Poison Resistance. They can be found in early
Act 2
areas.

Guardian of Solael
is a unique
Boss
creature that can be found in
The Hidden Path
near the
Broken Hills
.

Runestone of Dreeg
to enter it's lair. Killing him is necessary for the quest as he drops the
Runestone of Solael
upon death.
