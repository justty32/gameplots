Category

Empire

Speed
Effects:

An Era II
technology
exclusive to the
Wild Walkers
which increases the
movement speed of armies by 2 if they start their turn with an enemy army in vision range. Unlocked by completing Chapter 3 of the Wild Walkers faction quest.
Mechanics
The enemy army must be visible in order for the bonus to apply. Armies that are invisible due to the
Stealth
or
Ghost Force
capacities
, or in the fog of war, or blocked by elevation, will not trigger the bonus.
There is currently a bug where the bonus is recalculated when a game is loaded, giving applicable units an extra 2
each time. This can be stacked infinitely, potentially allowing armies to move across the entire map in one turn.
Lore
Digging into the resources of their clan totem animals, Wild Walker units react with the speed of threatened beasts when enemy units are in sight.
