Forgotten

Though they are front line fighters, the Assassin excels in feints, stunts and quick knife work. It is said that they can get you in the dark and from behind, even under the desert's noonday sun.

Shadowbinder is a

Where the twin blade Shadowbrand cuts brutally, stabbing at an enemy's vulnerabilities, Shadowbinder swings gracefully, carving fluid arcs through air and flesh and bone. When used together, the grace of Shadowbinder improves the defensive abilities of its wielder.
