An Era II
technology
exclusive to the
Broken Lords
which gives 1
for every XP earned by units or heroes on the empire. It is unlocked by completing Chapter 4, part 1 of the Broken Lords faction quest.
The Dust Crematorium is a major part of the Broken Lords' aggressive play style, as it makes winning combat extremely profitable: battles in the mid game can reward hundreds of dust, easily growing to thousands later. This is especially beneficial when playing against higher
difficulty
AI factions which can produce a seemingly, ahem, endless number of units.

Lore
Battlefield salvage teams bring the Broken Lords dead to crematorium sites where their living essence and traces of Dust can be extracted.

The tooltip description is misleading: the +1% regeneration only counts the number of pacified

The Sighing Scepter of Auir is a
Talisman
exclusive to the
Broken Lords
, received for completing Chapter 6 part 1 of their faction quest. The item is essential for the completion of Chapter 7 part 1 of the Broken Lords faction quest, which requires an army of 8 (6 if using a custom faction with the Anarchists
trait
) units all equipped with the Scepter. Fortunately, this task is made much easier due to the reward for completing Chapter 6 being 60 of the strategic resource the Scepter requires.
The reason that there are two different Scepters shown is because there is a random chance to end up with one or the other, and determines which versions of Chapter 6 and 7 of the Broken Lords faction quest the player gets. While the only difference in gameplay between these two paths is the Scepter itself, there are slight differences in the name, description, summary, and outcome text of the quests.
Lore
If you place your ear on this white-blue metal rod you will hear something. Is it echoes of a distant bell, the keening of lost souls, or merely the wind?
