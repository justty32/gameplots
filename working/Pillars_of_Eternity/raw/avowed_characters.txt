In 2781 AI, Maxim orchestrated a mass exodus from Spirento, taking with him a large number of the city's animancers along with their libraries and academic resources. He later wrote of the founding with satisfaction in his essay
Flower of Winter
, framing the settlement not merely as a migration but as a living revolution, proof that a community could thrive free of Vailian control.
The settlement of
Fior mes Ivèrno
flourished rapidly, its animancers driving agricultural and scientific development. Over time Maxim stepped back from direct authority, allowing the community to self-govern. By 2823 AI, Maxim expressed contentment with his legacy and confidence in his son
Ignasi Vidarro
, to whom he left leadership of Fior.
Maxim's later years were spent in contemplative solitude, during which his relationship with Skaen evolved into something more philosophical, finding communion with the Effigy not as a force of terror but as a catalyst for change. He discovered some
Godless
ruins near the settlement during this period which acted as a retreat for his musings, and his remains can be found there.

”
~
An excerpt from the

Sargamis
is a
dawn godlike
and an oracle of
Eothas
' in Eora. He appears in
Avowed
.
Background
Sargamis is a Dawnstar a spiritual manifestation, messenger, and servant of the deity
Eothas
.
As a direct manifestation of the god of light and dawn, his appearance reflects it. His skin shimmers with gold, his face is adorned with luminous symbols, and his eyes radiate a solid sun-like yellow light.
According to an Eothasian legend, Sargamis, along with
Eadnung
(the Right Hand) and
Modegund
visited the young
Waidwen
, a vorlas farmer living in a remote part of the colony of
Readceras
.The divine trio conveyed Eothas' message to Waidwen, instructing him to punish the local
Ardyran
governor for leading the people astray. This divine intervention ultimately ignited the
Saint's War
.
After the events at
Ukaizo
, he lost contact with his god, which was once made possible through a special dagger known as the Splinter of Eothas. In an attempt to regain the connection, Sargamis built a giant statue of Eothas, a reliquary to house the Splinter and amplify the spiritual connection.
Interactions
This character is
involved in
quests
.
Dawntreader
This character has
other interactions
.
He refers to the Envoy as "Brother/Sister/Cousin", as godlike are the "children of the Gods".
Quests
Dawntreader
: He tasks you with retrieving
The Splinter of Eothas
, a dagger so he can imbue a soul into a lifeless stone giant. It's kept within a shrine deep within the temple complex.
Behind the scenes
He is voiced by
Matthew Mercer
, who has voiced serveral characters in the
Pillars of Eternity
franchise, notably the companions
Aloth Corfiser
and
Edér Teylecg
.
Sargamis, Eadnung, and Modegund were originally mentioned in the
Book of Verses
, an Eothasian text found in
Pillars of Eternity
, as well as in
The Reaping
a short story by Eric Fenstermaker.
Sargamis was first seen in the
NVIDIA DLSS 3 & Ray Tracing Premiere
trailer,
in an open area at the foot of a giant statue. His name was first mentioned a few days later, in a blog post on Xbox Wire during Gamescom 2024,
. He's central to
Dawntreader
, a quest in the demo that was shown to media during the event.
He is the first example of a
dawn godlike
in the franchise, a kith who was blessed by
Eothas
before birth. Dawn godlikes are mentioned in the
TTRPG
, but do not appear in either
Pillars of Eternity
or
Deadfire
.

PAGE: The Envoy
CATEGORIES: Avowed characters
URL: https://pillarsofeternity.fandom.com/wiki/The_Envoy
======================================================================

The Envoy in the
Eastern Overlands
.
The Envoy of Aedyr
(also
The Envoy
or
Envoy
) is the
protagonist
and the
player character
in
Avowed
.
Biography
As the player character, the Envoy's history is primarily determined by the player during
character creation
. This includes details like how they rose to prominence within the court of
Aedyr
, and what their life was like before the events of the game, which are represented by their background, origin, and culture. Similarly, some of the details of their life
during
the time period of the game is determined by player choices, and no two players will have exactly the same experiences.
However, certain aspects remain consistent: the Envoy is a personal representative of the emperor of the
Aedyran Empire
, sent to the
Living Lands
to investigate rumors of a mysterious
plague
that has been corrupting the souls. As a threat to the Empire, the emperor wants the Envoy to uncover the source and put an end to it.
The Envoy is a
godlike
, a
kith
whose soul was blessed before birth by one of the
gods
. Typically a godlike will have a number of physical manifestations that echo the appearance of their patron. Unlike most godlike, the Envoy does not know what god they're attributed to, which is a mystery uncovered during the course of the game.
See also
The Watcher of Caed Nua
, the protagonist in
Pillars of Eternity
and
Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire
.

Background
“
Practically born with a grimoire in hand, Yatzli has been slinging spells and taunts her whole life. While she lives in Fior mes iverno with her loving partner, she's more often found in the ruins dotting the Living Lands.
However, just because she's in dusty ruins doesn't mean that she can't adventure in style. With a flash outfit and a signature sent in her wake, Yatzli relishes leaving an impression on all she meets, good or bad.

, with a particular interest in their artifacts, even if they're in hard-to-reach places. She has a lover in

who

Overwatch
.
Yatzli was first shown during the
Xbox Games Showcase Deep Dive
, on June 10, 2024, on the Official Xbox Podcast.
Game director Carrie Patel describes her as "a little lightning ball with a lot of personality," pointing out that she can get "a little risqué," and we see very quickly that her dialogue immediately takes a flirtatious tone.
On June 11, 2024, Obsidian released a
Yatzli Cosplay Guide
, featuring concept art by
Goodname Digital Art Studio
.
